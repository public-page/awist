Theme and Scopes

"Impact & Influence of Technology and Computing"

The transition of COVID-19 from epidemic to endemic has led to the rising numbers of innovations in technology and computing that had a significant impact and influence on people, either individually, in groups, institutions, communities, society, and even at the national levels. Since technology and computing have increasingly become networked, distributed, and accessible, the boundary of what is possible is only limited by our imagination.

This workshop aimed to establish a panel of discussion on the theme of impact and influence of technology and computing, both general and specific, in one discipline or multidiscipline, and either mixed, quantitative or qualitative types of research.

Thus, the scope of the topics include, but not limited to:

Health & Healthcare
Environment & Society
Acceptance & Sustainability
Entertainment & Well-Being
HCI & Interface Design
Accessibility & Digitalization
Information & Knowledge Management
Cyber Security & Awareness
Education & Pedagogy Support System
Learning & Instructional Design
Application Areas or Case Study
