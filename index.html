Theme and Scopes
Theme: “IMPACT & INFLUENCE OF TECHNOLOGY AND COMPUTING”

The transition of COVID-19 from epidemic to endemic had leads to the rising numbers of innovation in technology and computing that had an important impact and influence to people, either individually, groups, institution, community, society, and even at the national levels. Since technology and computing had increasingly become networked, distributed, and accessible, the boundary of what possible is only limited by our own imagination.



This workshop aimed to establish a panel of discussion on the theme of impact and influence of technology and computing, that both general and specific, in one discipline or multidiscipline, and either mixed, quantitative or qualitative types of research. Thus, the scope of the topics include, but not limited to:
- Health, Environment, Society
- Entertainment, Well-Being, Sustainability
- HCI, Accessibility, Digitalization
- Software Design, Knowledge Management, Cyber Security
- Learning, Education, Pedagogy Support System
- Application Areas or Example Case Study

Submission Guidelines

Workshop submission should be a 7~10-pages manuscript (in Springer LNCS format and which is a single-column format and the content focuses on one particular research problems, motivations, targeted objectives and achievements, which includes the following information:
• Title (informative and concise) and author’s information (single-blind review)
• Introductory (overview, objective) with brief literature comparison
• Proposed Approach (described with sufficient details)
• Results, Discussion, and Conclusion (main findings, arguments, prospects.)
• List of references
