Author's Submission Guidelines

The paper should be written with the consideration of the review process to be double-blind, properly organized, relevant to the AWIST scopes, sufficient levels of scientific contribution, and a good command of the English language.

Workshop submission should be a 7~10-pages in length (in Springer LNCS format) and which is a single-column format and the content focuses on one particular research problems, motivations, targeted objectives and achievements, which includes the following information:

  • Title (informative and concise) and authorâ€™s information (single-blind review)
  • Introductory (overview, objective) with brief literature comparison
  • Proposed Approach (described with sufficient details)
  • Results, Discussion, and Conclusion (main findings, arguments, prospects.)
  • List of references

Don't forget to check out the theme and scopes first over on the Call for Papers page.
