Tentative Program Schedule*

AWIST 2022 is tentatively planned to be held at the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST) on 14 and 15 December 2022. The two-day event will contain a keynote speech from our honored guests, followed by paper presentations by participating authors. Download the program book below (available soon).
